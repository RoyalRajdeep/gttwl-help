## Products in CMS

#### How to Get products from the cms 
---
1. Login into your agency admin.
2.  In the navigation bar, click *"Settings"* and select *"System Settings"* from the drop down.
3. Select the field beside the green *GO* button and select, from the drop down menu, *"Create a Custom Setting"* then click the green *GO* button.

Write `"sync_products"` into the *"Name of Setting"* field

Inside the content box write the names of sources, from the list below, for products you wish to receive. They are to be separated only by commas and no space.

#### List of sources
---
* *Gadventures*
* *Collette*
* *Globus*

*Please not the we only have one source at the moment.*
